Turn your WhatsApp or Telegram messages into clean, structured spreadsheets. Instantly.

Messy text? Voice notes? Order updates? Just send it — Chat2Sheet uses AI to format, extract, and log it into your Google Sheet.

How It Works

1. Message It

Send any message via WhatsApp or Telegram. No formatting rules needed.

2. Let AI Do the Work

3. See It in Your Sheet

Everything is neatly logged in real-time to your connected Google Sheet.
